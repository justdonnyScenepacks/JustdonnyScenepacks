ABOUT ME

I’m 18 years old, and I started editing when I was 16. What began as a simple interest quickly turned into a real passion that helped me grow in ways I never expected. Over the past two years, I’ve developed my skills in editing and expanded my journey by creating a Discord community, making YouTube videos, and producing After Effects tutorials. I also started creating scenepacks, which eventually led me to build my own website. My goal has always been to help others learn and improve in After Effects by sharing the knowledge and experience I’ve gained along the way. Everything I know today comes from constant practice, experimenting, and growing through editing. I never imagined I would reach 10K followers, but once I did, everything changed. It motivated me to push myself even further and create even better content than before. My journey started with editing football content, especially inspired by Feyenoord, and from there it all began. And this is just the beginning.
